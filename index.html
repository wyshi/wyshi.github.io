I am an assistant professor at Northeastern University, jointly appointed by ECE and Khoury Computer Science. I am broadly interested in NLP, Human-AI Interaction, and AI safety. Specifically, I investigate AI-driven persuasion, aiming to harness its societal benefits while mitigating its potential harm.

Research Projects

  • How AI Persuades Humans: PersuasionForGood, Cicero in Diplomacy, Anti-scam Chatbot, Movie Recommendation
  • How to Persuade AI for Safety: Persuasive Jailbreaker, LLMs Encodes Harm and Refusal Separately
  • AI's Societal Impact: Donation Persuasion, CultureBank
  • Conversational AI: Learning through Interaction, User Sentiment Adaptation
  • Privacy in AI: Selective Differential Privacy, Privacy Awareness in AI Agents
